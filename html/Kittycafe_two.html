Hello Kitty Cafe Truck

Hello Kitty Cafe on the road! The original Hello Kitty Cafe Truck, launched at Hello Kitty Con 2014, began as a sweet idea to offer supercute treats to fans in Southern California. Today, there are two Hello Kitty Cafe Trucks bringing smiles and treats to fans and friends across the US!
